Vision Sensor

A robots vision sensors detect the world around them, possibly through cameras or other sensor types.

Wheeled

Many robots have wheels are their way to move around the world. All of the mars rovers use wheels to move about the red planet.

X-ray

Among other sensors, robots can have x-ray cameras, such as spacecraft like Hubble.

Yaw

Movement around the y-axis. The movement of a factory robot arm on a base platform is the yaw.

Z-axis

The third dimension. The Z axis is perpendicular to the X axis to create 3D space.
