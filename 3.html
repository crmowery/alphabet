Lidar

Light detection and ranging. This is a form of radar that uses laser scans to create 3d point clouds of environments. The purpose of lidar in robotics is to allow the robot to understand it's surrounding environment and move around.

Missiles

In terms of robotics, many missiles are considered robots. Smart bombs, such as cruise missiles, are automatically controlled with intensive robotic systems to hit their targets.

Nanobot

Robots on a micro scale, theoretically used to alter the human body or move about in places unreachable normally.

Overclock

In computing, overclocking is when the computer is programmed to run faster than is considered safe for use. Overclocking allows the computer, or robot, to potentially process faster but at a cost of much higher wear and tear on the computer.

Pneumatic

A system that uses air as a the driving force for motion. Air muscles are tubes filled with air that expand and contract as air flows in and out of them, mimicking human muscles.
