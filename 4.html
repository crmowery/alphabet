Quadrupedal

A being that uses four appendages to move about. A robot with four legs would be quadrupedal.

Repair

Fixing devices. In robotics, repairing is essential for keeping robots like automatic factory arms fully functioning.

Spacecraft

All space satellites and vehicles are robots in some form. Spacecraft that are launched into deep orbit to capture imagery of other planets are considered robots.

Terrestrial

Existing on land. This refers to anything that moves about on land, specifically earth. The majority of robots are terrestrial, although there are aquatic robots.

UAV

An unmanned aerial vehicle. These robots take surveillance footage around the world.
